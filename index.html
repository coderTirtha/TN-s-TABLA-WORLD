About Us

Our journey began on 27 December, 2020.

TN's TABLA WORLD is a music channel, which was founded on 27 December, 2020 by Tirtha Das. Basically it is a channel based on song cover. On this channel, we try to cover various songs. Our first cover was "SHIVA TANDAV STOTRAM".
